Link to open " Big trees, fire and oldgrowth modelling " ArcGIS online map in full screen mode for best viewing. This is a mashup map containing widgets, analysis tools and a variety of layers availabe through various government data sites.


Link to open " Old Growth LiDAR visualisation " ArcGIS online scenemap globe as shown below in full screen mode for best viewing. This is a 3D visualistion of LiDAR data from Tasmania. Unfortunately there is not currently widely available LiDAR data for Victorian forests. This can be quite slow to load so please be patient! Once loaded please use mouse to toggle around and look at the data in 3D, scroll to zoom, left button to orbit and right button to pan. Link to LiDAR factsheet: Tas LiDAR Factsheet
